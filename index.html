WHO

Statisciacally there are more male cyberbullies than girls. This statistic aided by the fact that there are more males that play videogames than females. There are about up to 40% of indivisuals that would identify themselves of experiencing some form of cyberbullying. The reason for this is due to the anonimity of playing online in these games and, having diffrent aliases for diffrent websites and, usernames causes indivisuals to feel a sense of invulnerability of consequenses of what they say and do online.

WHAT

Cyberbullying is the act of intentionally harming someone emotionally & mentally through the use of the internet. There are many forms of cyberbullying with the main form being the sending of messages to a specific indivisual. But this act can scale anywhere from making a post about a specific person to sending them a direct message online to cause emotional distress or disease to an indivisual.

WHERE

Cyberbullying is of course most seen online throughout chatrooms, gaming, social networkds and more forms of communication between persons online. These interactions online can have offline effects throughout these victims lives.
